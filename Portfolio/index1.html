About Me

As a motivated and detail-oriented Mechanical Engineering graduate, I currently leverage my technical knowledge and practical exposure as a QC Engineer at HBL. My professional journey is marked by a strong foundation in industrial training, encompassing hands-on experience in Refrigeration and Air Conditioning (R&AC) systems, various welding techniques (Arc, MIG, Tig, Gas), and Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) methods. I possess a comprehensive understanding of workshop practices, safety protocols, and advanced mechanical maintenance techniques.

I thrive in collaborative environments, consistently striving to deliver innovative and cost-effective engineering solutions. My project experience, including the development of a multi-speed gearbox mechanism and a can crushing machine, has significantly honed my skills in mechanical design, precise force calculation, analysis of torque transmission, and optimizing power flow within complex mechanical systems. I am committed to a meticulous approach, always emphasizing robust teamwork and comprehensive documentation practices to ensure project success and quality.
